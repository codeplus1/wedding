The Chheka ceremony is a crucial step in the wedding process, signifying the families' mutual agreement and the formalization of the upcoming marriage. It's a moment of joy and celebration, marking the beginning of the wedding ceremony. It's a formal visit by the bride's family to the groom's home and They bring gifts for the groom and his family, which may include clothing, sweets, fruits, and other symbolic items. Matkor | Pre-wedding

The Matkor ritual holds a special place in the pre-wedding ceremonies, it is deeply rooted in our traditions. Matkor is a poignant reminder of our connection to nature and the importance of respecting the earth. It is a way of imploring the earth deities to bless the new couple with abundance. Date: 2082-01-03 BS | 2025-April-16 AD
